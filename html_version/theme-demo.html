Interactive Theme Switcher Demo

Click the palette icon in the top-right corner to switch between different dark mode themes. Your choice will be saved and applied across all pages.

How to use:
  1. Click the button in the top-right corner
  2. Choose from 6 different dark mode themes
  3. Your selection is automatically saved to localStorage
  4. Navigate to other pages to see the theme persists
  5. Click the palette button again to collapse/expand the switcher
